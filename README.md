# 🛍️ Vrinda Store — Annual Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive data analysis of **Vrinda Store**, an online retail business that sells products across multiple e-commerce platforms. The goal is to uncover actionable insights about sales performance, customer demographics, and platform effectiveness to help the store grow its business in 2023.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- **Microsoft Excel** — Data cleaning, processing, and analysis
- Excel features used: Pivot Tables, Charts, Filters, Data Validation, and Formulas

---

## 🔄 Data Workflow

```
Raw Data  →  Data Cleaning  →  Data Processing  →  Analysis  →  Dashboard / Insights
```

### Data Cleaning Steps
- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing/null values
- Standardized column formats (dates, categories, text)
- Corrected inconsistent entries (e.g., gender labels, state names)

---

## ❓ Business Questions Solved

| # | Question |
|---|----------|
| 1 | Compare sales and orders using a single chart |
| 2 | Which month recorded the highest sales and orders? |
| 3 | Who purchased more — men or women — in 2022? |
| 4 | What were the different order statuses in 2022? |
| 5 | Which are the top 5 states contributing to sales? |
| 6 | What is the relationship between age and gender based on number of orders? |
| 7 | Which online platform contributed the most to sales? |

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- **Peak Month:** Identified the month with the highest combined sales and order volume.
- **Customer Gender:** Women accounted for a significantly higher share of purchases compared to men in 2022.
- **Order Status:** Breakdown of orders into statuses such as Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, and Refunded.
- **Top States:** The top 5 states driving the majority of revenue were identified and ranked.
- **Age vs Gender:** Adult women (30–49 age group) placed the most orders, making them the primary target audience.
- **Top Platform:** Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra were among the key platforms, with one leading in total sales contribution.

---

## 💡 Conclusion & Recommendations

> To improve sales, Vrinda Store should focus on:
> - Targeting **women aged 30–49** living in the **top 5 contributing states**
> - Running promotions and ads on the **highest-performing online platform**
> - Addressing high cancellation/return rates to improve net revenue
